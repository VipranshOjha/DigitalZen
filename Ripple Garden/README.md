## рҹҢұ Why this project exists

Most of my time is spent building systems, dashboards, models, and applications with very clear goals.

Ripple Garden was the opposite.

The idea was simple:

> What if clicking on a screen felt as calming as dropping a pebble into a still pond?

That question became a small experiment in generative art, interaction design, animation, and atmosphere.

The result is a digital space that exists purely to be experienced.

---

## рҹҺҜ What it does

Open the page.

Click anywhere.

A ripple appears.

Click again.

Another ripple expands across the water.

Soon the pond fills with overlapping waves, drifting lotus petals, floating flowers, soft ambient sounds, and occasional distant chimes.

Nothing is being tracked.

Nothing is being optimized.

The experience simply unfolds as long as you choose to stay.

## вңЁ Features

### Water & Motion

* Multi-ring glowing ripple system
* Additive ripple blending and overlap effects
* Smooth 60 FPS animation
* Responsive full-screen pond
* High-DPI / Retina support

### Living Environment

* Floating lotus flowers with gentle bobbing motion
* Drifting lotus petal particles
* Dynamic sparkle effects
* Night Pond and Dawn Pond themes
* Aurora, Sunset, Sakura, and Emerald ripple palettes

### Immersive Audio

* Continuous pond ambience
* Interactive water sounds on ripple creation
* Random ambient wind chime events
* Master audio controls and volume adjustment

### Creative Tools

* One-click screenshot export
* Keyboard shortcuts for quick interaction
* Zero setup required
